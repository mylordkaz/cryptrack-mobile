# Privacy Policy for Cryptrack

**Last updated: March 10, 2026**

## Overview

Cryptrack does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All portfolio data, transactions, and preferences are stored exclusively on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. No accounts or sign-ups are required. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

## Third-Party Services

Cryptrack fetches publicly available cryptocurrency price data and exchange rates to display current market values. No personal information is sent in these requests.

## Security

Your data can be protected with biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID) or a PIN code. This security is handled entirely on-device.

## Analytics & Tracking

Cryptrack does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or any other telemetry.

## Children's Privacy

Cryptrack does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 years of age.
